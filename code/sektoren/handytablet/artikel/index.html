iPad Air 2022

All information about the new iPad Air 5. generation!

There are many rumors about the new iPad that has been announced last month. The iPad Air of the 5. generation comes with some stunning thing we want to discuss here

Specs

The iPad Air 5 transcends beyond Apple's A-series iPhone chips and packs Apple's very first custom silicon — the M1. M1 equips the iPad Air with an 8-core CPU, 8-core GPU, and 8GB of RAM for unrivalled performance. The new iPad Air is only the second iPad ever to come with an M1 chip under the hood, following in the footsteps of last year's iPad Pro. The iPad Air 5 also features a new 12 MP ultra-wide FaceTime camera on the front, 5G connectivity, and a 10.9-inch Liquid Retina Display.

Faster USB-C Connectivity — According to Apple, the USB-C port on the new iPad Air boasts up to 2x faster transfer speeds.

Center Stage — The iPad Air now supports Center Stage functionality for video calls, just like the iPad Pro and iPad mini.

Touch ID — The new iPad Air comes with Apple's uber-secure Touch ID, built right into the Power button.

Support for Accessories — Apple's 2022 iPad Air is compatible with the 2nd-generation Apple Pencil, Magic Keyboard, Smart Keyboard Folio, and Smart Folio cover.

Availability

You can order the new iPad Air starting Friday, March 11, with the official launch slated for Friday, March 18. The 2022 iPad Air is available with either 64GB or 256GB of internal storage in the following colourways: space gray, starlight, pink, purple, and a stunning new blue.
